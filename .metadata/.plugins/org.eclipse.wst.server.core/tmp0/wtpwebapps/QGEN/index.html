QGEN: Automating Exam Cells in Colleges

Vision and Mission
QGEN is a cutting-edge project with a bold vision and mission to revolutionize exam cells in colleges. Our aim is to automate the entire process of generating question papers in a computerized way, making examinations more efficient and streamlined.

Admin Control
As the controller of examinations, the Admin plays a pivotal role in the project. They have the authority to oversee all aspects of the system and manage the individuals involved in the project. Staff members play a crucial part in QGEN's operation. They can be added, removed, or modified by their respective admins to ensure smooth functioning.

Faculty Empowerment
Faculty members are vital employees who are entrusted with the responsibility to add and remove students from the system. Additionally, they have the privilege to create and modify question banks, from which random questions will be selected for students' examination Faculty can choose to allocate time for examination, course code, course name, and the number of questions to display in the question paper.

Student Access
Once students receive their credentials from the faculty, they can log in to the system. The Student page provides them with a convenient view of their grades, credits, and other important information. Students can access question banks prepared by faculty to understand how to answer specific questions.

Tailored Examinations
Faculty members have the flexibility to allocate examination time, specify course codes and names, and determine the number of questions to display in the question paper. This customization ensures that examinations are tailored to meet the specific requirements of each course. At QGEN, we strive to empower educational institutions and enhance the examination process through automation and advanced technology. Join us on this exciting journey towards a future where exams are conducted efficiently, fairly, and securely.
